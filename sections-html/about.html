02 WHO WE ARE

Where undergrads explore tomorrow's skies

UAMU is a discussion sandbox for students curious about advanced air mobility. We host conversations, share ideas, and connect across disciplines — laying the groundwork for bigger things ahead: design competitions, tech projects, and Canada's aerial future.

Multidisciplinary Team
From aerospace to design to systems engineering — our team bridges fields and fuels breakthroughs.
Driven by Purpose
We're not just building cool tech. We're solving real problems and enabling safer, smarter movement.
Community-Centered
Education, mentorship, and inclusion are core. We're here to uplift, not just invent.
